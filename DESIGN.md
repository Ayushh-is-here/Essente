## Brand & Style

This design system embodies an "Organic Sophisticate" aesthetic—a refined fusion of high-end culinary artistry and raw nature. It targets a discerning audience that values transparency, quality, and tranquility. The brand personality is professional yet warm, avoiding the sterile nature of traditional corporate design in favor of a "garden-to-table" narrative.

The visual direction is rooted in **Modern Minimalism** with **Tactile** influences. It utilizes generous whitespace to allow professional photography to act as the primary visual anchor. The style is characterized by:
- **Organic layering:** Using soft, tinted shadows and overlapping elements to create depth.
- **Refined Precision:** Clean lines and structured typography that suggest an artisanal attention to detail.
- **Intentional Restraint:** Every element serves a purpose; decorative flourishes are replaced by high-quality materials (photography, color depth, and type).
- **Iconographic Purity:** Strict adherence to minimalist SVG line icons to maintain a premium feel, replacing all casual imagery with structured symbols.

## Colors

The color palette is a sophisticated "Cilantro" spectrum. The foundation is built upon **Warm Cream** (`#F9F8F6`), which provides a softer, more organic canvas than pure white. 

- **Primary Forest (`#1B3022`)**: Used for high-contrast text, primary navigation backgrounds, and grounding elements.
- **Muted Sage (`#8DAA91`)**: Serves as the primary functional color for secondary actions, borders, and soft accents.
- **Subtle Gold (`#C5A059`)**: Reserved for "moments of delight"—special offers, star ratings, or delicate borders that signify premium quality.
- **Cilantro Main (`#4a7c42`)**: The interactive driver for buttons and active states, maintaining the brand's namesake vibrance.

Color is applied with a focus on hierarchy; darker greens anchor the content, while the lighter, pale greens create soft containers that recede into the cream background.

## Typography

This system utilizes a classic Serif/Sans-Serif pairing to balance heritage with modernity. 

**Playfair Display** is the "Brand Voice." It is used for all major headings and titles to evoke a sense of tradition and editorial elegance. It should be used with tight letter-spacing in larger sizes to maintain its high-fashion feel.

**Inter** is the "Functional Voice." It provides a crisp, neutral contrast for body descriptions, menu details, and UI labels. The high x-height and clean apertures of Inter ensure maximum legibility at small sizes, particularly for ingredient lists and nutritional information.

Typography scales are optimized for a "spacious" feel. Body text should prioritize line height (1.5–1.6) to prevent the layout from feeling cramped, ensuring the "clean" brand promise is met.

## Layout & Spacing

The layout philosophy follows a **Fixed Grid** model on desktop to maintain a controlled, gallery-like presentation. Containers are centered with a maximum width of 1400px, creating wide gutters that emphasize the premium nature of the brand.

**Key Layout Rules:**
- **Vertical Rhythm:** A base 8px unit dictates all spacing. Sections are separated by large gaps (80px+) to ensure a relaxed, non-hurried browsing experience.
- **Responsive Reflow:** 
  - **Desktop (1024px+):** 12-column grid, generous margins.
  - **Tablet (768px - 1023px):** 8-column grid, reduced 30px margins.
  - **Mobile (<767px):** Single-column stacked layout. Headlines should switch to `-mobile` variants to maintain readability without excessive wrapping.
- **Card Grids:** Menu items utilize a standard 3-column (desktop) or 2-column (tablet) grid with 24px gutters, allowing images to be the focal point.

## Elevation & Depth

Visual hierarchy is established through **Tonal Layers** and **Ambient Shadows**. Instead of harsh black shadows, this system uses tinted shadows that incorporate the primary green hue, creating a more cohesive and natural depth.

- **Level 1 (Base):** The Cream (`#F9F8F6`) background.
- **Level 2 (Cards):** White surfaces (`#FFFFFF`) with a subtle tinted shadow: `0 4px 20px rgba(27, 48, 34, 0.08)`. Used for menu items and content cards.
- **Level 3 (Interactive/Hover):** Lifted state with a more pronounced shadow: `0 12px 30px rgba(27, 48, 34, 0.12)`.
- **Level 4 (Overlay):** Modals and dropdowns use a semi-transparent background blur (backdrop-filter) alongside the Level 3 shadow to suggest a physical layer of glass above the UI.

Depth is also suggested through "Ghost Borders"—1px solid lines using `cilantro-pale` or `muted-sage` at low opacity (10-20%) to define boundaries without adding visual weight.

## Shapes

The shape language is **Rounded**, reflecting the organic theme of the café. Sharp corners are avoided to maintain a welcoming and "soft" aesthetic.

- **Standard Cards:** 1rem (16px) corner radius for a friendly but structured look.
- **Buttons & Pills:** Full pill-shaped (100px) or 0.5rem (8px) depending on the context. Primary "Order" buttons should use the pill shape for maximum distinctiveness.
- **Imagery:** Food photography should use the standard card radius (16px) or, for hero sections, can occasionally feature one or two custom rounded corners (e.g., top-left and bottom-right at 4rem) to create an organic, custom-cut feel.

## Components

### Buttons
- **Primary:** Forest green background, white text, pill-shaped. On hover, transition to Cilantro Main with a slight upward lift (2px).
- **Secondary:** Transparent background with a Muted Sage border (1px). Text in Forest green.
- **Tertiary/Ghost:** Text-only in Forest green with a subtle gold underline on hover.

### Inputs & Selects
- Fields use the Cream background with a 1px border in `cilantro-pale`. 
- Focus state: Border changes to `muted-sage` with a subtle green outer glow.
- Labels: Always use `label-sm` (Inter, uppercase, bold).

### Cards (Menu Items)
- White background, 16px radius, Level 2 shadow.
- Image at the top, followed by a 16px padding container for the Title (Playfair) and Description (Inter).
- Price should be highlighted in `cilantro-main` using Inter Bold.

### Chips/Tags
- Used for dietary labels (e.g., "Vegan", "Gluten-Free"). 
- Small, pill-shaped, with a very light sage background (`#e8f5e3`) and Forest green text. No icons; text only.

### Iconography
- Strictly minimalist SVG line icons (2px stroke). 
- Avoid any filled or illustrative styles. 
- Icons should be Forest green or Gold depending on their importance.
